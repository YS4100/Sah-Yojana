FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How to apply for the schemes?

Visit the schemes page from the home page and click on the yojana tab visible at the top right navigation bar. Further click on the apply button to apply for the selected scheme.

How to view the guidelines?

Visit the yojana page to get the info about the scheme. You can also watch the video to know about the guidelines and the eligibility.

How to know if I am eligible for the yojana?

Based on the details you provide in the profile you will get the recommendations of the yojana's on the yojana page.

Would I be able to know the recommended yojana without creating an account?

No, you won't be able to know the recommended yojana's without creating account. After login/sign up you need to fill up the profile details after which you will be suggested yojana's based on your eligibilty.
